What is DEVCON

DEVCON is one of the premier developer conference happening in Kerala every year for some time. Aptly titled K-MUG DEVCON 2016 is happening on December 3, 2016 at the Zentih Hall, UST Global in Bhavani, Technopark, Thiruvananthapuram. DEVCON 2016 will help you to engage and collaborate with Microsoft MVPs and developers, build connections and learn about the Microsoftâ€™s cutting edge technologies from the industry experts.
DEVCON is one of the largest and popular developer conference of it's kind in Kerala, attracting a lot of tech professionals every year. Conducted by Kerala Microsoft Usergroup(K-MUG) every year, thousands of attendees have benefited from the platform since the event began in 2010.
K-MUG Developer Conference is an event where developers and architects from all walks of the tech life meet and listen to talks given by an assortment of distingushed speakers. This year's event will see speakers from Microsoft, Microsoft MVP's, Architects from MNC companies and Enterprenuers to share their knowledge and expertise with you. Scheduled for December 3rd, 2016, this year's event makes a comeback to the place where it all started. Technopark, Trivandrum the world's greenest Technopolis and largest IT Park in Kerala will be hosting the event at Zenith Hall in the Bhavani Building.

About KMUG

K-MUG is one of the most successful technical community in India, which focus on Microsoft Technologies. Microsoft recognized the efforts made in building the community by selecting K-MUG as the Best Developer User group for Tier 2 cities as part of the Community Impact Awards for 2010.
K-MUG is active community based out of Kerala which started its activities around 8 years ago and regularly conducts events across Kerala targeting both professionals and students. Normally K-MUG conducts monthly usergroup meeting in the third week of the month and you view more details about the past events in the EventBrite Site.
